# Example 3 — Swarm under one Grant: budgets bind authority, not architecture
Five planner/executor agents share grant grt_X (budget: 50 actions/day, SAR 20,000/day).
They emit 50 mixed sentences in parallel; sentence 51 (any of them) receives
`budget_exhausted` BEFORE side effects. The System never saw — and never needed to see —
the swarm's internal conversation. Authority attached to the Grant, not to the heads. ✅ G5, speaker-neutrality
